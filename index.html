Welcome to Leva Patel Samaj (Nerul)

We are a non profitable community organisation run by our most experienced seniors and energetic young members who are focused on providing educational, cultural, social, welfare and medical needs of the large Leva Patel community living around the world.

Samaj also have a community centre having Leva Patel community members living in Navi Mumbai since long who are always happy to help our community and their connivance in respect of whether be accommodation for students who are seeking higher education in top universities and those people who came for medical causes.

We will be happy to have opinions from our community members. And with getting more and more closer, let’s all make more efforts to serve better future to our community.
